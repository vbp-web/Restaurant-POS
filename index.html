About RestroPOS

Our Story & Mission

Transforming Restaurant Management

RestroPOS is a comprehensive, production-ready Point of Sale (POS) system designed specifically for modern restaurants. Built with cutting-edge technologies like Electron, React, and Node.js, we provide a seamless native desktop experience that works across Windows, macOS, and Linux.

Why We Built This

We understand the challenges restaurant owners face daily - from managing orders and inventory to tracking staff performance and analyzing sales data. That's why we created RestroPOS: a powerful, all-in-one solution that simplifies restaurant operations while providing the insights you need to grow your business.

Our Commitment

We're committed to continuous innovation and providing exceptional support to our customers. With features like QR ordering, real-time analytics, smart billing, and subscription-based pricing, we make professional restaurant management accessible to businesses of all sizes.
